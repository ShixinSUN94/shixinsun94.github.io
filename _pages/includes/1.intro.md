Shixin Sun received the B.E. from Henan University of Technology, Zhengzhou, China, in 2018, and the M.S. from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, Beijing, China, in 2021. He is currently working toward the Ph.D. with the Faculty of Computing, Harbin Institute of Technology, Harbin, China. His research interests include perception, video generation and end-to-end model for autonomous driving.
