Shixin Sun received the B.E. degree from Henan University of Technology, Zhengzhou, China, in 2018, and the M.S. degree from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, Beijing, China, in 2021. He is currently working toward the Ph.D. degree with the Faculty of Computing, Harbin Institute of Technology, Harbin, China. His main research interests include Multi-Modal Perception, End-to-End Models, and Street Scene Generation for Autonomous Driving.
